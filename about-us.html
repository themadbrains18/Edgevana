A journey of a thousand miles begins with a single step.

Edgevana was founded in 2019 by Mark Thiele & Subhan Jahromi with the express intent of disrupting the existing complex processes of deploying one or 1000’s of data center/edge locations.

Edgevana is at the forefront of helping companies create new business opportunities and improve customer engagement through the effective use of global Edge solutions.

Regardless of size or complexity, Edgevana is dedicated to building the solution thatbest fits your enterprise needs atand the most effective cost.
